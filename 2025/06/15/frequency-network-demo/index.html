A Frequency Bandwidth Extension Network Applied To Bone-Conducted Speech

Posted on 2025-06-15 Edited on 2025-06-16 In Speech Enhancement

Author: Dianzhe Ding
Email: dingdianzhe@mail.ioa.ac.cn

Introduction

We present some speech samples in the website to show the proposed frequency bandwidth extension network. Objective and subjective evaluations on A4BS dataset show that our system substantially outperforms existing bone-conducted speech enhancement systems.
