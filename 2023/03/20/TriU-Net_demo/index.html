IOA-Audio

Audio & Speech Research Group is a research group under the Key Laboratory of Noise and Vibration Research, Institute of Acoustics, Chinese Academy of Sciences. The Principal Investigator of this research group is Prof. Feiran Yang. Our research interests include Blind Source Separation, Array Signal Processing, Voice Anti-Spoofing, Acoustic Echo Cancellation, Speech Enhancement.
